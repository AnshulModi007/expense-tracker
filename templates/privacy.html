Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Spendly ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use the Spendly expense tracking service.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data: Amounts, categories, dates, and notes you enter into the app.
  • Usage data: Basic information about how you interact with the Service (e.g., pages visited, actions taken) to help us improve functionality.

We do not collect payment card details or financial account credentials of any kind.

3. How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Operate and maintain your Spendly account.
  • Display your expense history and generate spending summaries.
  • Send account-related communications such as password resets.
  • Improve the Service based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard measures, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed password storage. We retain your account and expense data for as long as your account is active.

If you delete your account, your personal data and all associated expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third Party Services

Spendly does not integrate with or share your data with third-party analytics, advertising, or data broker services. The only external resources the Service loads are:

  • Google Fonts: Used to load typefaces for the interface. Google may log the font request; please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

We do not embed social media trackers, advertising pixels, or any other third-party scripts.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Export your expense data in a portable format.

7. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie solely to keep you logged in between page loads. We do not use tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though doing so will prevent you from staying signed in.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you via the email address associated with your account.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact details provided within the app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
